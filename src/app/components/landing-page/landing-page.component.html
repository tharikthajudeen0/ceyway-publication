Project Overview

CEYWAY is an AI-powered mobile application revolutionizing tourism in Sri Lanka by providing real-time personalized itinerary generation.

By integrating live data such as weather, traffic, events, and crowd density, the app generates optimized travel plans tailored to each user's preferences and budget.

With advanced AI and intuitive design, CEYWAY supports flexible transportation modes and unifies fragmented travel data into a seamless smart platform.

Research Problem

Tourists face fragmented and inefficient trip planning due to scattered information across multiple platforms, leading to confusion and errors.

Current travel apps focus narrowly on single features without integration or real-time adaptation, missing personalization and timely updates.

Lack of comprehensive transport planning and real-world budgeting assistance leaves travelers juggling between unreliable sources.

No existing smart, AI-driven, real-time platform consolidates travel info, causing a major gap in the Sri Lankan tourism experience.
