Project Overview

AI Meets Smart Tourism

CEYWAY is an AI-powered mobile application built to revolutionize tourism in Sri Lanka by delivering real-time, personalized travel itineraries.

‚ÄúPersonalization is no longer a luxury‚Äîit's an expectation.‚Äù

The app dynamically integrates data such as weather conditions, and local events to tailor travel plans specific to each user's preferences and budget.

Combining AI intelligence with intuitive UI design, CEYWAY supports multiple modes of transport and bridges the gap between scattered tourism data sources, offering a unified and intelligent travel planning experience.

Research Problem

Tourists face fragmented and inefficient trip planning due to scattered information across multiple platforms, leading to confusion and errors.

Current travel apps focus narrowly on single features without integration or real-time adaptation, missing personalization and timely updates.

Lack of comprehensive transport planning and real-world budgeting assistance leaves travelers juggling between unreliable sources.

No existing smart, AI-driven, real-time platform consolidates travel info, causing a major gap in the Sri Lankan tourism experience.
